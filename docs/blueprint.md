# **App Name**: PhysioGuide

## Core Features:

- Homepage with information: Landing page detailing services offered, target conditions, and approach to physiotherapy.
- Therapist Profiles: Introduce the team with bios, qualifications, and specializations, pulled from links like https://beacons.ai/dr.caleb.burgess?utm_source=ig&utm_medium=social&utm_content=link_in_bio&fbclid=PAb21jcAPVafxleHRuA2FlbQIxMQBzcnRjBmFwcF9pZA81NjcwNjczNDMzNTI0MjcAAafxiYrOJTvHBkt3qhSCvQDUL0ES9rjlIiCnS0cyeAox04vceG3eRJaGiPYGuw_aem_CSwfl0axybrTMNoFiRv1HQ.
- Condition Information: Pages detailing various conditions treated, with symptoms, causes, and treatment options (possibly AI-generated summaries based on up-to-date medical knowledge). Content can be derived from sources like recoverpain.com/fix.
- Treatment Guides: Provide detailed guides on specific physiotherapy exercises and techniques for patients to follow at home, using images and videos.
- Appointment Booking: Allow patients to book appointments online, view availability, and manage their bookings.
- Contact Form: Simple contact form to allow potential patients to ask additional questions or request specific information.
- AI-Powered Exercise Suggestion Tool: Based on a user's description of their pain and limitation of movement, provide suggested exercises, acting as a helpful tool (disclaimer: not medical advice).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#4169E1), reminiscent of health and trust. 
- Background color: Light bluish-white (#F0F8FF), almost a pure white for a clean, clinical feel, but with a hint of the primary color.
- Accent color: Violet (#8F00FF), provides a modern, sophisticated contrast and draws attention to interactive elements.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' (serif) for headlines, and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent services and conditions, maintaining a professional look.
- Ensure a clear, intuitive layout with easy navigation. Prioritize readability and visual hierarchy to guide users effectively.
- Subtle animations and transitions for a smooth user experience, such as fade-ins and sliding effects on page load or content changes.
